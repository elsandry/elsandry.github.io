About me

I’m Eleanor Sandry. Welcome to my winding path of thinking and posting at Zigzaggeries.

I was a Senior Lecturer in Digital and Social Media / Internet Studies at Curtin University. Alongside my teaching, where I could find time, I was a researcher in human-machine communication and, to an extent, I’m planning to continue that research outside of the university environment. I’m mainly interested in non-humanoid robots, which I most often find in creative art and fictional contexts.

For me, robots have the most potential to be interesting and valuable in human lives if they are explicitly different from humans, with nonhumanlike ways to act in and on the world.

I’m aware though that I might not do much research or writing about robots for a while. I need a break. You’ll probably find me writing about my physical garden (as opposed to this digital garden), keeping my houseplants alive, sewing, cooking and golf.
