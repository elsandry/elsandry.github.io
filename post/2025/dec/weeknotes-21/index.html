Weeknotes 15 - 21 December 2025

 21 December 2025

A slightly disgruntling week…

…where I did manage to hold together a few things (but not golf on Friday)!

  • Cover image: not good quality, but didn’t want to spook the very hot and thirsty kookaburra making use of the garden amenities.
  • Continued Putting Tiny Experiment with practice at home and club. Probably only helpful if extended (potentially over many years), but feels like a good way to develop skills with clear ideas and notes.
  • Successfully baked Marzipan Fruitcake Cookies! Highly recommend the linked blog as a good read and great source of cooking inspiration.
  • Had a couple of disappointing golf days in spite of good practice sessions. Snatched defeat from the jaws of victory on Friday. Eventually got furious with myself and bailed on lunch to lick my wounds. Trying to focus on the excellent first nine (but failing so far).
  • Nominating 3-5 best shots from Friday would almost certainly help (not done yet)!
  • Also annoyed with the way I keep not going wing-SUPing (although it had mostly been too windy).
  • Lots of meditation and weights helps.
  • Actually watered the plants and recut the frangipani cuttings into more manageable pieces.
